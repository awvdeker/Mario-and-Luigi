ABOUT US

The founders

We (Mario and Luigi) both started as plumbers. But after years of building, fixing and cleaning pipes for other people, we realized that we only have one life and that it was time to follow our forever dream: opening our own restaurant in Ghent. Now here we are. As big bro Mario often says: "Ah spaghetti. Ah, ravioli. Ahh, mama mia." So come and try it by yourself!

The team

We'd be nothing without our dedicated team of cooks, kitchen aids and waiters at your service. Peach, Donkey, Toad, Yoshi... Don't hesitate to ask them for suggestions. Tell them too if you have food allergies or intolerances, we'll be happy to adjust.
